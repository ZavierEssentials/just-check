About

Ikemefune Franklyn Nnamdi is a content creator, comic, recording artiste, copywriter, content developer & tech enthusiast. A graduate of Political Science from the prestigious University of Ibadan. Over the past 5 years, Frank has worked in various capacities in the entertainment industry. From winning the Hennessy VS class national rap competition to releasing 4 music projects to critical and commercial acclaim, to partnering on ads with brands like Hennessy, Pepsi, Supa Komando, Orijin, to name a few; ... to working on campaigns for Tech Companies like Dillali, Kaoshi Network, Skill Paddy, LearnAm, Lagos State Resident Registration Agency, to name a few; to collaborating with notable content creators on social media like Mr. Macaroni, Legal Juggernaut, Agba (Koni baje baby), Inspector K. Frank has also worked as Head Of Content at WeTalkDigital, which is a creative agency that provides services from design to PR and marketing; to music artistes and record labels in Africa. Through WTXtra, he has worked with Empire, Teni The Entertainer, BadBoyTimz, Aristokrat Records, Tiwa Savage, Timaya, Reminisce etc. Frank is also the individual behind the viral internet series “If An Agbero Had A Diary”, where he plays Samo Agbero. The series had a total of 2.1 million views across Instagram, TikTok and Twitter within its first month of release. He was also a guest speaker & panelist at the Tech Summit Ogun State 2022. An event that also had in attendance the Honorable Minister for Communications and Digital Economy, Dr. Isa Pantami.
